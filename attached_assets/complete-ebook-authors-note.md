# Author's Note

I grew up in Baptist and Methodist churches. I was baptized, attended Sunday school, went to revivals. I did the things you're supposed to do.

But even as a child, something didn't fit.

The message I was given and the visuals I saw didn't match. The words said one thing; the imagery suggested another. And the answers I received when I asked questions always ended the same way: "You're not supposed to understand everything. That's what faith is for."

I was told to imagine a fence with something on the other side. All I had was a knothole to peek through. I was supposed to see just enough to build faith in what I couldn't fully comprehend, then live my life based on that glimpse.

But that never made sense to me.

How could a loving Creator - one who made us above the angels, in His own image, with the ability to create - give us only partial information? How could He expect us to guess our way through the most important decision of our existence? The idea that the Father would play hide-and-seek with eternal truth seemed absurd. It didn't match the character of the being described in the very scriptures I was told to trust.

So I pushed away. Tried to be my own god. Fell flat on my face. Over and over. Studied with different denominations. Found some that made more sense than others. But none of them made total sense. None of them answered the question that had haunted me since childhood.

Until now.

After decades of confusion, addiction, and searching - and after finally clearing my mind long enough to receive something beyond my own noise - the dots connected. Not through religious dogma. Not through institutional doctrine. Through careful study, honest questioning, and suddenly... clarity.

What I'm presenting in this book is not an attack on Christianity. It's not heresy designed to create a flashpoint. It's not me trying to change your mind.

It's simply what made sense when I finally allowed myself to look.

---

Here's what I need you to understand before you continue:

The confusion is growing. Theories are becoming more wild and incredible by the day. People are waiting for a man to step onto the world stage who will be "the Antichrist" - a single figure, easily identified, who will demand worship and reveal himself as evil.

But what if the deception has already happened?

Scripture says the mark of the beast will be in the forehead and in the hand. The forehead is where you think - your beliefs, what you hold in your heart. The hand is what you do - your works, your actions.

What if "accepting something into your heart" and "doing works in its name" IS the mark? Not a chip. Not a barcode. Something organic. Something spiritual. Something millions have already taken willingly, believing they were doing right.

Scripture also says that in the last days, people will stand before the throne and say: "Lord, Lord, we prophesied in your name, we did mighty works in your name." And the response will be: "Depart from me. I never knew you."

Why would that happen? If they did everything in His name?

Unless the name was wrong.

I'm not saying this to frighten you or condemn you. I'm saying it because the scripture itself raises the question - and the mainstream explanations never satisfied me. They never explained how "even the elect would be deceived" unless the deception was dressed up to look exactly like the truth.

---

The Father's name - Yahuah - appears over 6,800 times in the original Hebrew scriptures. It was replaced with "LORD" and "GOD" - titles, not names. The Messiah came "in the Father's name" - Yahusha literally means "Yahuah is salvation." The name contains the Father's name within it. That's what "coming in his name" means.

But those names were changed. Translated. Modified. Until they no longer carried the original frequency.

I'm not claiming to have all the answers. I may be paraphrasing when I should be quoting directly. I may be connecting dots in ways that others would connect differently. But for me, this is what made sense when nothing else ever did.

The first volume of this book presents the evidence - the history, the patterns, the documented substitutions and erasures that built the system we live in today.

The second volume is my testimony - what happened when I finally allowed myself to step through the fear and look at what was on the other side.

I'm not trying to convert you to anything. I'm not starting a new religion. I'm not asking you to abandon your faith.

I'm asking you to consider whether the faith you were given has been corrupted - and whether the truth that was always there has simply been veiled.

If what I'm saying resonates, explore it. If it doesn't, set it aside. That's between you and your own discernment.

But if you've ever felt that nagging sense that something doesn't fit - that the answers you've been given don't fully satisfy - then maybe this is for you.

The confusion has to stop. The clarity that I'm starting to experience should be available to everyone, because it's what we were supposed to have all along.

All glory to the Father, Yahuah. All honor to the Son, Yahusha.

They cared enough to offer redemption. This is my attempt to share what I believe that redemption actually looks like - stripped of the additions, the fear, and the control that was layered on top of it.

Read with an open mind. Judge for yourself.
