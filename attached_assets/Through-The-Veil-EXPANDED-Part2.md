# CHAPTER 5: THE FLOOD AND WHAT IT DESTROYED

The flood was necessary because the corruption had spread so far that barely anyone remained untainted. Bere'shiyth (Genesis) 6:5-7: "And Yahuah saw that the wickedness of man was great in the earth, and that every imagination of the thoughts of his heart was only evil continually. And it repented Yahuah that he had made man on the earth... And Yahuah said, I will destroy man whom I have created from the face of the earth."

But it wasn't just a water event. It was a reset. A wiping of the slate.

## The Universal Flood Memory

Flood narratives appear in virtually every ancient culture on Earth. This isn't coincidence - it's corroboration:

**Mesopotamian Accounts**:
- The Epic of Gilgamesh (Tablet XI), discovered on clay tablets in the ruins of Nineveh in 1853, describes Utnapishtim surviving a global flood by building a boat at divine command
- The Atrahasis Epic (dated to approximately 1700 BCE) contains a detailed flood account
- The Sumerian Flood Story featuring Ziusudra predates both

**Greek and Roman**:
- Deucalion and Pyrrha survived a flood sent by Zeus, landed on Mount Parnassus
- Ovid's "Metamorphoses" describes the flood in detail

**Indian**:
- The Matsya Purana describes Manu being warned by a fish avatar of Vishnu about an impending flood
- The Shatapatha Brahmana contains a similar account

**Chinese**:
- The Gun-Yu flood narrative describes a great flood during the reign of Emperor Yao
- Recent geological studies published in Science (2016) found evidence of a catastrophic flood on the Yellow River around 1920 BCE

**Native American**:
- The Hopi speak of three world destructions, one by water
- The Ojibwe flood narrative involves Nanabozho and a great flood
- The Aztec describe four previous worlds destroyed, one by water (Atonatiuh)

**Pacific Islands**:
- Hawaiian traditions describe a flood survived by Nu'u on a large canoe
- Tahitian, Samoan, and Maori traditions all contain flood narratives

Dr. James George Frazer documented over 500 flood traditions in his work "Folk-Lore in the Old Testament" (1918). These stories come from cultures with no contact with each other, yet share common elements: divine warning, one family saved, boat construction, animals preserved, a mountain landing.

## The Geological Evidence

Conventional geology explains Earth's sedimentary layers through uniformitarianism - slow, gradual processes over millions of years. But the evidence also supports catastrophism:

**Marine Fossils at High Altitudes**:
- Marine fossils are found on mountain peaks worldwide, including the Himalayas
- The Burgess Shale in the Canadian Rockies (7,000+ feet elevation) contains remarkably preserved marine fossils
- Conventional explanation: mountains rose slowly over millions of years
- Alternative: a global flood deposited these creatures and the mountains formed afterward

**Polystrate Fossils**:
- Fossilized trees that extend through multiple sedimentary layers (supposedly representing millions of years)
- Found in coal mines in Tennessee, Nova Scotia, and throughout Europe
- If the layers took millions of years to form, how did the trees remain upright without decaying?
- More consistent with rapid deposition from a catastrophic flood

**The Genesis Flood Model**:
- Dr. John Baumgardner, geophysicist at Los Alamos National Laboratory, developed a computer model (TERRA) showing how catastrophic plate tectonics could produce observed geological features through a global flood event
- The model explains rapid continental movement, volcanic activity, and sedimentary deposition

**Fossil Graveyards**:
- Mass burial sites containing jumbled bones of multiple species are found worldwide
- The Karoo Formation in South Africa contains an estimated 800 billion vertebrate fossils
- The Green River Formation in Wyoming contains billions of fish fossils, many showing signs of rapid burial
- These graveyards suggest catastrophic, not gradual, burial

## What Was Lost

Consider what was lost. An entire civilization. Technology that had been taught by the Watchers. Structures. Knowledge. History.

**The Antediluvian World**:
- Lifespans of 900+ years allowed for accumulation of knowledge impossible today
- The Book of Jubilees describes advanced cities and culture before the flood
- Enoch describes structures and technology that shouldn't have existed

**Post-Flood Changes**:
The post-flood world was fundamentally different:
- **Atmospheric changes**: The "waters above the firmament" (Genesis 1:7) may have created a different atmospheric pressure and UV protection, explaining pre-flood longevity
- **Lifespan decline**: The genealogies show exponential decay from Noach's 950 years to Avraham's 175 to Mosheh's 120 - a curve consistent with accumulating genetic damage after removal of protective conditions
- **Geographic changes**: Continental separation may have occurred during or after the flood (Genesis 10:25 - "in his days was the earth divided")

## The Eight Survivors

And consider who survived. Noach and his family. Kepha Ri'shon (1 Peter) 3:20 confirms: "Eight souls were saved by water."

Eight people carrying forward whatever they had preserved - genetically, spiritually, intellectually:
- **Noach** - righteous, blameless in his generations

- **Shem, Ham, Japheth** - the three sons from whom all nations descended

The Table of Nations in Genesis 10 traces all peoples back to these three sons:
- **Shem** - ancestor of Semitic peoples (Hebrews, Arabs, Assyrians)

- **Japheth** - ancestor of European and Asian peoples

DNA research has confirmed that all humans share common maternal and paternal ancestors - mitochondrial Eve and Y-chromosomal Adam. The science confirms what scripture records.

## The Pattern of Reset

The pattern of conquest is always the same: defeat the enemy, erase their memory, take their children, tell them a new story about who they are.

This is what happened at the first reset. And it would happen again.

# CHAPTER 6: DRAGONS, NOT DINOSAURS

The word "dinosaur" was invented in 1842 by Sir Richard Owen. He coined the term from Greek: "deinos" (terrible) + "sauros" (lizard). Before that, the creatures we now call dinosaurs were called something else.

Dragons.

## The Etymology and Historical Record

**Pre-1842 Documentation**:
The word "dragon" appears in virtually every ancient language:
- Hebrew: **tanniyn** (תַּנִּין) - appears 28 times in the Hebrew scriptures
- Greek: **drakon** (δράκων) - from which we get "dragon"
- Latin: **draco**
- Old English: **draca**
- Chinese: **lóng** (龍) - a category including various types

**Dictionary Definitions**:
Up until around 1946, Webster's Dictionary defined dragon as "a fabulous animal, generally represented as a monstrous winged serpent or lizard, with a crested head and large claws, and regarded as very powerful and ferocious."

Note: "fabulous" at that time meant "from fables" or "notable" - not "fake" or "imaginary." The 1828 Webster's actually described them as "now rare" - implying they still existed but were uncommon.

## Scriptural Documentation

Scripture speaks of dragons as real creatures, not metaphors:

**Iyov (Job) 41** describes the leviathan in specific physical detail:
"Out of his mouth go burning lamps, and sparks of fire leap out. Out of his nostrils goes smoke, as out of a seething pot or caldron. His breath kindles coals, and a flame goes out of his mouth. In his neck remains strength... His heart is as firm as a stone; yea, as hard as a piece of the nether millstone."

This description includes:
- Fire/flame from the mouth
- Smoke from nostrils
- Armored scales
- Extreme strength
- Aquatic habitat (sea creature)

**Iyov (Job) 40:15-24** describes behemoth:
"Behold now behemoth, which I made with you; he eats grass as an ox. Lo now, his strength is in his loins, and his force is in the navel of his belly. He moves his tail like a cedar: the sinews of his stones are wrapped together. His bones are as strong pieces of brass; his bones are like bars of iron."

Key details:
- Made "with" Job - contemporary, not ancient
- Herbivore
- Tail like a cedar - massive, not small like a hippo or elephant
- Strongest of God's creatures
- Lived near rivers

Modern translations try to identify behemoth as a hippopotamus or elephant - but neither has a tail "like a cedar." A sauropod dinosaur, however, matches the description exactly.

**Other Dragon References**:
- Yesha'yahu (Isaiah) 27:1: "Yahuah with his sore and great and strong sword shall punish leviathan the piercing serpent... and he shall slay the dragon that is in the sea."
- Yesha'yahu (Isaiah) 13:22: "And the wild beasts of the islands shall cry in their desolate houses, and dragons in their pleasant palaces."
- Yirmeyahu (Jeremiah) 51:34: "He hath swallowed me up like a dragon."
- Tehilliym (Psalm) 74:13: "Thou didst divide the sea by thy strength: thou brakest the heads of the dragons in the waters."

## Global Dragon Traditions

The Chinese zodiac includes a dragon alongside eleven real animals: rat, ox, tiger, rabbit, snake, horse, goat, monkey, rooster, dog, and pig. Why would one imaginary animal be included among eleven real ones?

Dragon accounts appear across every culture:

**European Dragons**:
- St. George and the Dragon (3rd-4th century accounts)
- The Lambton Worm of England
- The Lindworm of Scandinavia
- Specific dragon-slaying accounts in local histories

**Asian Dragons**:
- Chinese dragons with specific types and characteristics
- Japanese ryū with distinct behaviors
- Korean yong
- Vietnamese rồng

**Americas**:
- Quetzalcoatl - the feathered serpent of Mesoamerica
- The Piasa Bird - documented by Father Jacques Marquette in 1673 near present-day Alton, Illinois
- Thunderbird traditions across Native American cultures

**Africa**:
- The Mokele-mbembe of the Congo - still reported in modern times
- The Ninki-nanka of West Africa

**Middle East**:
- The Sirrush of Babylon, depicted on the Ishtar Gate (now in Berlin's Pergamon Museum)

These accounts share specific physical details: scales, fire or poison breath, flight capability in many cases, reptilian features. They're not describing the same single animal - they describe regional variations with consistent core characteristics.

## The Dinosaur Substitution

The dinosaur narrative serves a purpose: it supports the millions-of-years timeline that contradicts scripture.

Consider the sequence:
1. Dragons documented across all cultures for thousands of years
2. 1842: Richard Owen coins "dinosaur" and begins reconstructing creatures from bones
3. Darwin's "Origin of Species" published 1859
4. Gradual replacement of dragon accounts with "primitive mythology" dismissal
5. Dinosaur extinction dated to 65 million years ago - conveniently before humans
6. Dragon accounts reinterpreted as exaggerated crocodile or snake encounters

The effect: human witness testimony spanning millennia is dismissed as superstition, replaced with reconstructions from bones that can be interpreted multiple ways.

**Soft Tissue Discoveries**:
In 2005, Dr. Mary Schweitzer (paleontologist at North Carolina State University) published findings of soft tissue, blood vessels, and red blood cells in a Tyrannosaurus rex femur. This was published in Science magazine.

Soft tissue cannot survive 65 million years. The maximum documented survival of proteins is thousands of years, not millions. Yet soft tissue has now been found in multiple dinosaur specimens:
- T. rex (Montana)
- Hadrosaur (Montana)
- Triceratops (Montana)
- Various specimens in China

The implications: either the specimens are not millions of years old, or our understanding of organic preservation is fundamentally wrong.

## The Connection Forward

Here's the thread connecting forward: if dragons were real and were rebranded as dinosaurs, what else was rebranded? What else was substituted?

The pattern appears again and again:
- Eyewitness testimony dismissed as myth
- Scientific framework constructed to replace it
- Anyone questioning the framework labeled as ignorant or anti-science
- The original knowledge erased within a generation or two

Dragon legends may be accurate memories of real creatures from previous ages. The creatures themselves may have been destroyed as part of the judgment against the corruptions created by the Watchers. Or they may have simply been hunted to extinction in the post-flood world.

Either way, the rebranding serves the larger narrative: humanity is new, the universe is random, scripture is mythology. Once you accept the dinosaur timeline, you've accepted a framework that makes the rest of scripture seem absurd.

# CHAPTER 7: THE DOG-HEADED BEINGS AND HYBRID REMNANTS

St. Christopher, according to multiple historical accounts, was the dog-headed saint. Not allegory. Not artistic license. Documentation.

## The Historical Record of St. Christopher

The Eastern Orthodox tradition preserves icons of St. Christopher with a dog's head. These aren't isolated examples:
- Byzantine icons from the 11th-17th centuries (multiple examples in Greek and Russian collections)
- The St. Christopher icon in the Byzantine Museum of Athens
- Manuscript illuminations in the British Library
- Icons in Russian Orthodox monasteries

The Western church eventually suppressed this imagery. In 1969, Pope Paul VI removed St. Christopher from the universal Roman calendar (though not from veneration entirely). The dog-headed imagery was largely abandoned in the West centuries earlier.

**The Historical Accounts**:
Multiple ancient sources describe dog-headed races:
- **Ctesias of Cnidus** (5th century BCE) - Greek physician and historian, described the Cynocephali (dog-heads) of India in his work "Indica"
- **Herodotus** (5th century BCE) - mentioned dog-headed men in Libya
- **Pliny the Elder** (1st century CE) - described Cynocephali in his "Natural History"
- **Marco Polo** (13th century) - reported dog-headed inhabitants on the Andaman Islands
- **Ibn Battuta** (14th century) - described similar beings in his travels
- **The Travels of Sir John Mandeville** (14th century) - extensive descriptions of dog-headed races

These reports span over a thousand years and come from educated observers - historians, physicians, clergy, explorers.

## The Cynocephali in Christian Tradition

The story of St. Christopher in some traditions:
- Born a Cynocephalus (dog-headed being)
- Sought to serve the greatest king
- Eventually converted to Christianity
- Used his great size and strength to carry travelers across a dangerous river
- One day carried the Christ child, who revealed himself as the King of all creation

The name "Christopher" means "Christ-bearer" - commemorating this event.

**Theological Implications**:
If the dog-headed tradition is accurate, it suggests:
- Non-human intelligent beings existed alongside humans
- They were capable of communication and reason
- They could receive salvation through Christ
- The church preserved this memory until it became inconvenient

## Pre-Flood Hybrid Origins

These beings may have been remnants of pre-flood hybrids or fallen experiments.

The Book of Jasher (referenced in Joshua 10:13 and 2 Samuel 1:18) describes the corruption before the flood:

"And the sons of men in those days took from the cattle of the earth, the beasts of the field and the fowls of the air, and taught the mixture of animals of one species with the other, in order therewith to provoke the Lord." (Jasher 4:18)

This suggests genetic manipulation - the mixing of species - was part of the pre-flood corruption. The Nephilim weren't the only abominations. Chimeras - beings combining human and animal characteristics - were created.

**Supporting Evidence**:
- Egyptian gods depicted with animal heads (Anubis - jackal, Horus - falcon, Sobek - crocodile)
- Mesopotamian lamassu - winged bulls with human heads
- Greek mythology filled with hybrid beings (centaurs, minotaurs, satyrs, harpies)
- Hindu traditions include Narasimha (lion-man) and Ganesha (elephant-headed)
- Native American traditions include various shapeshifters and animal-human beings

These aren't random imaginations. They're consistent memories across unconnected cultures of beings that actually existed.

## Explorer Accounts

There are historical reports - very real reports by explorers - of encounters with non-human intelligent beings:

**The Andaman Islands Reports**:
Marco Polo described the inhabitants of the Andaman Islands as dog-headed, though this has been reinterpreted as misunderstanding or racism. The actual indigenous Andamanese people are fully human, but the islands may have harbored other populations.

**African Reports**:
European explorers in Africa reported encounters with beings that didn't fit human categories. Many of these reports were dismissed as racism or misunderstanding, but some contain details that don't fit that explanation.

**The Blemmyes**:
Ancient accounts describe the Blemmyes - headless beings with faces on their chests - inhabiting regions of Africa. These appear in:
- Herodotus' "Histories"
- Pliny's "Natural History"
- Medieval mappae mundi (world maps)
- Shakespeare's "Othello" references them

Dismissed as fantasy, but the consistency across centuries of reports from unconnected sources suggests something was being observed.

## The Cannibalism Question

Historical accounts note that these beings, while intelligent and capable of communication, were reportedly cannibalistic under certain conditions. This aligns with the Enochic description of Nephilim behavior - consuming human flesh when other food was scarce.

The pattern of cannibalism among the corrupted beings appears in:
- Enoch's description of the giants
- Greek titan mythology (Kronos devouring his children)
- Aztec accounts of gods requiring human sacrifice
- Various island cultures reporting giant cannibals in their ancestral traditions

## The Thread Continuing

The thread continues: remnants of the corruption survived. Not just in bloodlines. In actual beings that were documented before the documentation was discredited.

The strategy is consistent:
1. Beings exist and are documented by observers
2. Documentation is preserved for centuries
3. "Enlightenment" arrives and documentation is reinterpreted as myth
4. Anyone who takes the accounts literally is labeled primitive
5. Within a generation, the memory is erased

St. Christopher with the head of a dog might not be symbolic of transformation or spiritual journey. It might be a literal record of what existed before the purge was complete.
