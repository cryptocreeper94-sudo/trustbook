The tablets date to approximately 1400-1200 BCE and contain striking parallels to the Hebrew scriptures.

In Mesopotamian tradition, the Anunnaki formed a council of deities who determined the fates of humanity. The Sumerian King List, preserved on the Weld-Blundell Prism (now housed in the Ashmolean Museum, Oxford), records rulers who lived for tens of thousands of years before a great flood reset civilization. # CHAPTER 2: THE 200 WATCHERS DESCEND

The Book of Chanok (Enoch) describes two hundred angels who abandoned their station in heaven. They descended to Mount Hermon. They took human women as wives. They taught forbidden knowledge. They fathered giants.

## The Textual Evidence

This wasn't myth to the early church. The text was considered authoritative scripture by significant portions of the early church:

**Yahudah (Jude) 1:6** references it directly: "And the angels which kept not their first estate, but left their own habitation, he has reserved in everlasting chains under darkness unto the judgment of the great day."

**Yahudah (Jude) 1:14-15** directly quotes 1 Enoch 1:9: "And Enoch also, the seventh from Adam, prophesied of these, saying, Behold, the Lord comes with ten thousands of his saints, to execute judgment upon all..."

**Kepha Sheniy (2 Peter) 2:4** confirms: "For if Elohim spared not the angels that sinned, but cast them down to hell, and delivered them into chains of darkness, to be reserved unto judgment."

The Greek word used here is "Tartarus" (ταρταρόω) - the only time it appears in scripture. In Greek mythology, Tartarus was a prison for the Titans - beings who existed before the Olympian gods. Another parallel that suggests common source memory rather than coincidence.

## The Dead Sea Scrolls Confirmation

The Book of Enoch wasn't just quoted in scripture - it was preserved. Among the Dead Sea Scrolls discovered at Qumran between 1947 and 1956, fragments of at least eleven copies of 1 Enoch were found, making it one of the most attested texts in the collection (only Psalms, Deuteronomy, Isaiah, and Genesis were better represented).

The specific fragments include:
- 4Q201-202 (4QEnocha-b)
- 4Q204-212 (4QEnochc-k)
- 1Q23-24 (1QEnGiantsa-b) - fragments of the Book of Giants, an Enochic work

The Aramaic fragments date to approximately 200-150 BCE, confirming the text predates Christianity and wasn't a later Christian fabrication.

## Mount Hermon: The Landing Site

The Book of Enoch specifically names Mount Hermon as the landing site of the Watchers. This is significant for several reasons:

**Geographic Significance**: Mount Hermon sits at the intersection of modern Lebanon, Syria, and Israel. At 9,232 feet, it's the highest point in the region. Ancient peoples consistently associated high places with divine access points.

**The Name Itself**: The Hebrew name "Hermon" (חרמון) derives from "cherem" - meaning devoted to destruction, or banned/cursed. The mountain's very name commemorates the oath the Watchers swore there.

**Archaeological Evidence**: The ruins of a temple sit at the summit of Mount Hermon, dated to the Hellenistic/Roman period but likely built on an older sacred site. Inscriptions found there reference "the oath." In 1869, British explorer Sir Charles Warren found a stone stele with an inscription reading: "According to the command of the greatest and holy god, those who take an oath proceed from here."

The Watchers didn't just descend anywhere. They chose a specific location that became permanently marked in both name and structure.

## Metallurgy: Technology Before Its Time

The teaching of metallurgy to early humanity presents a historical puzzle. Orthodox archaeology dates the Bronze Age to approximately 3300-1200 BCE and the Iron Age beginning around 1200 BCE. Yet anomalous artifacts appear that don't fit this timeline:

**The London Hammer**: Discovered in 1936 in London, Texas, embedded in rock claimed to be over 400 million years old. The iron hammer head has been examined by metallurgists who note the unusual purity of the iron and its resistance to corrosion.

**The Antikythera Mechanism**: Discovered in 1901 in a Roman shipwreck off the Greek island of Antikythera, this device is essentially an analog computer for calculating astronomical positions. Dated to approximately 100 BCE, it demonstrates a level of mechanical sophistication not supposedly achieved again until the 18th century. Over 30 bronze gears work together to track the cycles of the sun, moon, and planets.

**Out-of-Place Artifacts (OOPArts)**: Researchers like Michael Cremo have documented hundreds of anomalous artifacts that don't fit conventional timelines. His book "Forbidden Archaeology" (1993) catalogs evidence suggesting advanced technology existed in periods when humans were supposedly primitive.

If advanced knowledge was introduced by fallen beings rather than evolved through human ingenuity, then the history of technology needs to be reexamined.

# CHAPTER 4: THE NEPHILIM AND THE CORRUPTION

The Nephiliym - the giants of old - were the offspring of the Watchers and human women. Bere'shiyth (Genesis) 6:4 records: "There were giants in the earth in those days; and also after that, when the sons of Elohim came in unto the daughters of men, and they bore children to them, the same became mighty men which were of old, men of renown."

## The Etymology of Nephilim

The word "Nephilim" (נְפִילִים) comes from the Hebrew root "naphal" meaning "to fall." This directly connects them to the "fallen ones" - the Watchers. They are literally "the fallen ones' offspring."

Some scholars debate whether the root should be understood as "those who cause others to fall" (i.e., tyrants who bring down nations) or simply "those who have fallen" (offspring of the fallen angels). Both interpretations point to the same conclusion: these beings were connected to the celestial rebellion.

The Septuagint (Greek translation of the Hebrew scriptures, dated to approximately 250 BCE) translated "Nephilim" as "gigantes" - from which we get our word "giant." This wasn't a mistranslation. The translators in Alexandria understood the beings being described.

## Physical Evidence of Giants

Accounts of giant skeletal remains have appeared throughout recorded history:

**Newspaper Records (1800s-early 1900s)**: Hundreds of newspaper articles from the late 19th and early 20th century documented discoveries of giant skeletons across North America:
- The New York Times (December 25, 1868) reported a 9-foot skeleton discovered in Sauk Rapids, Minnesota
- The Worthington Advance (October 18, 1894) documented a 10-foot skeleton found during mining operations
- Various reports document skeletons with double rows of teeth - a detail that appears across multiple independent accounts

**The Smithsonian Controversy**: Multiple researchers have documented claims that the Smithsonian Institution collected and suppressed giant skeletal remains. The institution has denied this, but FOIA requests have produced incomplete records. The American Institution of Alternative Archaeology filed suit in 2014, resulting in court documents suggesting destruction of evidence, though the institution disputed these claims.

**Native American Oral Traditions**: Virtually every Native American tribe has oral traditions about giants who once inhabited the land:
- The Paiute speak of the Si-Te-Cah, red-haired giants who were cannibals
- The Navajo describe the Starnake, ancient giants killed by the ancestors
- The Choctaw tell of a race of giants called "Nahullo"

**The Lovelock Cave Discovery**: In 1911, miners extracting bat guano from Lovelock Cave in Nevada discovered mummified remains and artifacts, including sandals 15 inches long (suggesting a being approximately 8-10 feet tall). Red-haired human remains were found. Some of this material was initially collected by the Museum of Natural History but much was scattered or lost.

## The Genetic Corruption

The mixing of the angelic with the human produced something that violated the divine order. It wasn't just genetic - it was spiritual. The resulting beings were powerful but wrong. They had capabilities humans didn't, but they lacked something humans did.

The Book of Enoch describes the Nephilim consuming human flesh when food became scarce, and drinking blood. They were described as consuming 1,000 camels, 1,000 horses, and 1,000 sheep daily - emphasizing their enormous appetites and the devastation they brought.

**1 Enoch 7:3-5**: "And they became pregnant, and they bore great giants, whose height was three thousand ells: who consumed all the acquisitions of men. And when men could no longer sustain them, the giants turned against them and devoured mankind. And they began to sin against birds, and beasts, and reptiles, and fish, and to devour one another's flesh, and drink the blood."

The measurement "three thousand ells" is often debated - taken literally it would suggest impossible heights. More likely it's an ancient way of expressing "enormously tall" rather than a precise measurement. Other Enochic texts suggest heights of 300 cubits (450 feet) which is clearly hyperbolic, while Genesis implies heights more consistent with the skeletal evidence - 9 to 15 feet.

## Noah's Genetic Purity

The flood was necessary because the corruption had spread so far that barely anyone remained untainted. Bere'shiyth (Genesis) 6:9 tells us Noach was "perfect in his generations" - a phrase that deserves careful examination.

The Hebrew phrase is "tamim hayah b'dorotav":
- **Tamim** (תָּמִים) - complete, whole, without blemish, entire. This word is used elsewhere for sacrificial animals that must be without physical defect.
- **B'dorotav** (בְּדֹרֹתָיו) - in his generations/lineages

This may refer to genetic as much as moral purity. Noah's bloodline remained untainted by the Nephilim corruption when virtually everyone else had been compromised.

The Septuagint translates this as "teleios" - perfect, complete - and "en te genea autou" - in his generation/genealogy.
