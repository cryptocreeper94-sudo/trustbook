# CHAPTER 14: TARTARIA AND THE MUD FLOOD

Take a walk through any old city - Boston, St. Louis, San Francisco, Chicago, New York. Look at the buildings from the 1800s. Notice how many of them have windows at street level that are half-buried. Notice how many have "basements" that clearly were once ground floors.

## The Global Pattern of Buried Architecture

The official explanation is settling. Or rising street levels. Or intentional burial for utility access.

But the pattern is too consistent. Too widespread. Too global.

**Documented Examples in the United States**:

**San Francisco**: After the 1906 earthquake and fire, vast areas were rebuilt atop rubble. But photographs from before 1906 show buildings with buried ground floors - the phenomenon predated the earthquake.

**Seattle**: The city explicitly raised its streets 12-30 feet after the Great Seattle Fire of 1889. The "Seattle Underground" today consists of the original ground floors of buildings, now subterranean. Tours take visitors through these buried streets. But Seattle's documentation of raising its streets is rare - most cities don't have such explicit records.

**Chicago**: After the Great Chicago Fire of 1871, engineers raised the entire city by 4-7 feet using jackscrews. Buildings were lifted while business continued inside. This was presented as an engineering marvel to improve drainage. But photographs before the fire show buildings already partially buried.

**St. Louis**: Buildings in the downtown area show the characteristic half-buried windows. The old courthouse, constructed 1839-1862, has ground-floor windows at sidewalk level that were clearly designed for a different ground level.

**European Examples**:

**Edinburgh, Scotland**: The city has multiple underground levels, including the famous "Edinburgh Vaults" - a series of chambers in the arches of the South Bridge, completed in 1788. Within decades, they were abandoned and sealed due to flooding. The entire first floor of buildings along the Royal Mile is now underground.

**Paris, France**: The Catacombs of Paris contain the remains of approximately 6 million people, transferred from overflowing cemeteries in the late 18th century. But the tunnels themselves are far older - some sections date to Roman times, others to unknown periods.

**Rome, Italy**: The entire city sits atop multiple buried layers. Archaeological excavations consistently find floors below floors, streets below streets. The Roman Forum is approximately 30 feet below current ground level.

The theory gaining traction among researchers is that a previous civilization - sometimes called Tartaria, sometimes called the Old World Order - was more advanced than officially acknowledged.

- Abraham Ortelius (1570)

These maps show Tartaria as a major political entity with cities, provinces, and infrastructure. By the 19th century, Tartaria disappears from maps entirely. The official explanation: it was never a unified empire, just a European name for the nomadic peoples of the steppes.

But the architecture remains. Buildings in Russia, Eastern Europe, and Central Asia display the same ornate style - Roman columns, precise geometry, massive stone construction - that appears across Europe and the Americas.

**The Star Forts**:
Star-shaped fortifications appear on every continent except Antarctica. Officially, these were designed for cannon warfare - the angled walls would deflect cannonballs. But:
- Many star forts predate widespread cannon use
- The precision required seems beyond the technology officially available
- The global distribution suggests a unified architectural tradition
- Many are now buried or partially buried

Examples include:
- Fort McHenry (Baltimore, USA)
- Castillo de San Marcos (Florida, USA)
- Palmanova (Italy) - an entire star-shaped city
- Fort Bourtange (Netherlands)
- Numerous examples throughout the former Soviet Union

# CHAPTER 15: THE PETRIFIED GIANTS

Devils Tower in Wyoming is presented as a geological formation. An igneous rock formation created by cooling magma. Studied by geologists. Explained by science.

But look at it. Really look at it.

## Devils Tower Analysis

**The Official Explanation**:
Devils Tower formed approximately 50 million years ago when magma intruded into sedimentary rock. As the magma cooled, it contracted and cracked into hexagonal columns (columnar jointing). Over millions of years, erosion exposed the tower.

**The Alternative Observation**:
The vertical striations look exactly like bark. The overall shape looks exactly like a tree stump. The proportions match what would be expected if a massive tree - impossibly massive by current understanding - had been cut down.

The Native American Lakota, Cheyenne, and Kiowa tribes all have legends describing Devils Tower as a tree stump - or as the ground rising to protect children from a giant bear, whose claw marks created the striations.

**Scale**:
Devils Tower rises 867 feet from its base to summit. The summit is relatively flat, approximately 180 feet by 300 feet. If this were a tree, the diameter would be consistent with the height - proportional.

## Similar Formations Worldwide

Devils Tower isn't alone. Similar formations exist around the world:

**Giant's Causeway (Northern Ireland)**:
Approximately 40,000 interlocking basalt columns, mostly hexagonal. The official explanation is an ancient volcanic eruption. The columns look exactly like bundled plant fibers.

**Fingal's Cave (Scotland)**:
Columns identical to Giants' Causeway, forming a sea cave. The precision of the hexagonal columns seems too regular for random geological processes.

**Svartifoss (Iceland)**:
Another basalt column formation surrounding a waterfall. The same hexagonal pattern.

**Organ Pipes (California)**:
Columnar basalt formation in Inyo County, resembling organ pipes or... bundled plant fibers.

**Table Mountains/Tepuis (South America)**:
Flat-topped mountains in Venezuela, Guyana, and Brazil. Mount Roraima rises 9,219 feet with a nearly flat summit plateau. These look like stumps cut off at uniform height.

**Mesas of the American Southwest**:
Flat-topped formations throughout Utah, Arizona, and New Mexico display similar characteristics - vertical sides, flat tops, as if cut.

## The Giant Tree Theory

What if these are the remnants of a global forest? Trees that were miles high. Trees that would have touched the firmament. Trees that were cut down in the destruction that accompanied the reset.

**The Biblical Context**:
Scripture references great trees:
- The cedars of Lebanon were renowned for their size (2 Kings 14:9, Ezekiel 31)
- Ezekiel 31:3-9 describes Assyria as a great cedar whose "height was exalted above all the trees of the field"
- The tree in Nebuchadnezzar's dream reached to heaven (Daniel 4:11)

The Norse world tree Yggdrasil connected the nine worlds. The Tree of Life appears across cultures. What if these weren't just metaphors?

**The Petrification Process**:
Wood can become petrified when:
- Buried rapidly in sediment
- Water carrying dissolved minerals permeates the wood
- Silica (from volcanic ash) replaces organic material cell by cell
- The process can occur relatively quickly under the right conditions

Arizona's Petrified Forest contains trees buried by volcanic sediment approximately 225 million years ago (according to conventional dating). The trees are stone, but retain their original structure.

If a cataclysm buried massive trees in volcanic ash and sediment, rapid petrification could occur. The silicon-based rock would retain the original shape - including bark patterns.

## Evidence of Cutting

Some researchers point to what appear to be axe marks or saw marks on certain formations:

**The Flat Tops**:
Devils Tower, the tepuis, and many mesas have remarkably flat tops. Natural erosion tends to create uneven, weathered surfaces. The flat tops suggest cutting or breaking at a specific height.

**Consistent Heights**:
In some regions, multiple formations appear to be cut at the same height - as if everything above a certain level was removed.

**The Debris**:
If massive trees were cut, where is the debris? Perhaps scattered, buried, petrified, or removed. Perhaps the building blocks for structures we've attributed to human builders.

## The Implication

If something cut these trees down, it would have to be something capable of operating at a scale beyond current comprehension. Divine judgment? Angelic war? Something else entirely?

The point isn't to claim certainty. The point is to notice the pattern. To ask the questions that the official narrative doesn't permit. To consider that the world might have looked very different in a previous age - and that evidence of that age might be hidden in plain sight.

# CHAPTER 16: THE ORPHAN TRAINS AND MEMORY ERASURE

Between 1854 and 1929, an estimated 200,000 children were transported from East Coast cities to the Midwest and beyond on what were called "orphan trains."

## The Official Narrative

Charles Loring Brace, founder of the Children's Aid Society in New York City, initiated the orphan train movement in 1853. The stated purpose was charitable: remove children from urban poverty and place them with rural families who needed labor and could provide better lives.

**The Numbers**:
- Approximately 200,000-250,000 children transported
- Operating from 1854 to 1929 (75 years)
- Children sent to 47 states and Canada
- Average age: 10 years old
- Some as young as infants, some as old as 17

**The Process**:
Children were gathered from streets, orphanages, and sometimes families deemed unfit. They were cleaned, dressed, and loaded onto trains. At each stop, local families would inspect the children and select which ones to take. Siblings were often separated. Follow-up was minimal.

## The Questions

But the questions persist:

**Where did all these orphans come from?**
The official explanation cites:
- Immigration (parents died or abandoned children)
- Urban poverty
- Disease epidemics
- Parental death or abandonment

But the numbers seem disproportionate. 200,000+ children with no verifiable family connections, in a country with a total population of approximately 31 million in 1860?

**Why were records incomplete or missing?**
Many orphan train riders later searched for their origins and found:
- No birth certificates
- No record of their parents
- Incomplete or contradictory documentation
- Institutions that had "lost" their files

The Orphan Train Heritage Society of America has collected thousands of stories from descendants - a consistent theme is the impossibility of tracing origins.

**Why no memory of birth families?**
Even children old enough to remember (ages 5-12) often had no memory of parents, siblings, or home life before the orphanage. This suggests either:
- Trauma-induced amnesia
- Very young age at separation
- Something else

## The Population Replacement Theory

Some researchers suggest the orphan trains were population replacement. Children from a previous civilization - survivors of the reset - redistributed to families who would raise them with no knowledge of the past.

**The Pattern of Conquest**:
Throughout history, conquering powers have:

- Taken the children
- Raised them in the conqueror's ways
- Erased memory of the previous culture

Examples:
- Roman treatment of conquered peoples
- Spanish treatment of indigenous Americans
- Australian "Stolen Generation" (Aboriginal children removed from families)
- Canadian residential schools (Indigenous children forcibly assimilated)

If a reset occurred in the 1800s - if a previous civilization was destroyed or conquered - what would happen to the children? They would need to be redistributed, raised by families who knew only the new narrative.

## The Cabbage Patch Connection

During the orphan train era, a term emerged for these children with erased histories.

**The Folklore**:
When children asked where babies came from, European folklore told them babies were "found in the cabbage patch" or "delivered by storks."

For orphans with no traceable past - no family records, no birthplace, no origin story - the term took on a darker meaning. They might as well have sprouted from the ground.

**The Visual Evidence**:
Vintage postcards from the early 1900s - contemporaneous with the orphan trains - depict babies emerging from cabbages. German and French artists created these images extensively between 1900 and 1920.

A French silent film from 1896, "La Fée aux Choux" (The Cabbage Fairy), showed babies being harvested from a cabbage garden.

These weren't random artistic choices during an era when 200,000 children were being redistributed with no verifiable origins.

**The 1983 Phenomenon**:
Then in 1983, Cabbage Patch Kids dolls became a national phenomenon. Created by Xavier Roberts, each doll came with unique "adoption papers" and a birth certificate. The marketing emphasized that these dolls weren't purchased - they were adopted. Children participated in adoption ceremonies at retail stores.

The timing is notable. Just as historical inquiry into the orphan trains intensified (researchers in the 1970s-80s began examining records critically), a toy appeared that normalized the concept:
- Adoption papers became cute
- Being found rather than born became a playful narrative
- The darker history of children with erased identities was repackaged as something adorable

Whether intentional or coincidence, the effect was the same. A generation of children internalized a story: some people just appear. Some people are found rather than born to families. Some people have adoption papers instead of family trees.

## The Systematic Erasure

The orphan trains may have been exactly what conquering powers always do - on a civilizational scale:
1. Defeat the previous order
2. Erase their memory
3. Take their children
4. Raise them in the new story

The children who rode those trains became the grandparents and great-grandparents of today's Americans. Their lost memories became everyone's lost history.
