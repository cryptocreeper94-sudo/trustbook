# DarkWave Trust Layer - Social Media Content Roadmap

## Content Strategy Overview

**Goal:** Build sustained interest through a drip campaign that educates, intrigues, and builds FOMO without revealing competitive advantages too early.

**Platforms:** Facebook, Instagram, TikTok (Reels format)
**Frequency:** 1-2 posts per day across accounts
**Format:** Short reels with motion, visuals of the portal, stats overlays

## Content Rules

1. **Never reveal:** Multi-SIG multi-chain details, Academy acquisition funnel strategy, Business Tenant Portal specifics
2. **Always include:** Trust angle, verification benefit, protection value
3. **Tone:** Confident but not arrogant. Educational but not preachy. Urgent but not desperate.
4. **Visuals:** Motion always. Portal screenshots. Stats overlays. Premium feel.
5. **CTA variety:** Mix "link in bio" with softer calls like "something to think about"
