## Examples

**Example 1: Signup Only**
- 1 person signs up, does not buy
- You earn: 1,000 Shells

**Example 2: Signup + Small Purchase**
- 1 person signs up and buys $10
- You earn: 1,000 (base) + 5,000 (bonus) = **6,000 Shells**

**Example 3: Signup + Big Purchase**
- 1 person signs up and buys $100
- You earn: 1,000 (base) + 50,000 (bonus) = **51,000 Shells**

**Example 4: Multiple Referrals**
- 10 signups, 3 buy $25 each, 1 buys $100
- Signups: 10 × 1,000 = 10,000 Shells
- 3 × $25 buyers: 3 × 10,000 = 30,000 Shells
- 1 × $100 buyer: 1 × 50,000 = 50,000 Shells
- **Total: 90,000 Shells = 9,000 SIG = $90 at TGE**
